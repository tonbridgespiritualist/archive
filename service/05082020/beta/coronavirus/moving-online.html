Moving Online

Published 23 May 2020

From: Office of the Secretary and Jay Bragg

Due to the Pandemic, the Centre was forced to close because we were unable to provide the required social distance of 2m and the ban on gatherings imposed by the shutdown.

We are now moving online to serve our community, via Zoom. These sessions will take place on Sunday evenings. You will be emailed details at 5:30pm, provided you are signed up to the mailing list. The meeting will open at 6pm and we will begin at 6:30pm.

How it works

When you join the Zoom call, you will be admitted into a waiting room. If you join before the service starts, you will see a slideshow of information. When the service begins you will be muted.

When we begin, you will see Lisa open the service and then the medium will take over.

If you are chosen for a message, you can record it. On the bottom bar of your Zoom Meeting (on PC) you should see the option to Record, on mobile you can record using your usual phone screen recording settings. We will also record incase you lose your recording, these will be held in accordance with our Privacy Policy, available here.

What You Need

1. Zoom Video Conferencing software (on your phone, tablet or computer)

Note: On your phone and tablet, the app is ZOOM Cloud Meetings

2. A stable internet connection

3. To be signed up on our form below and confirm your email.

Rules

1. Please don't have any rude or offensive backgrounds (whether real or virtual) or you risk being ejected from the meeting.

2. Please don't be disrespectful if you receive a message, if you don't want a message please politely decline.
