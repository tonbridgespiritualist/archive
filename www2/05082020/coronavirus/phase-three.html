Phase Three: Bring It All Back

We are preparing appropriate solutions to bring back your much loved services such as refreshments and the raffle.

Published 16 July 2020

From: Office of the Secretary and Jay Bragg

We want to bring back your favourite services such as the Raffle and Refreshments as soon as possible. But we want to do so in a way that keeps you safe.

Raffle

Our raffle is our most popular service. Our current plan to bring it back is as follows:

  • Payment for your ticket would be added to your donation.
  • It would be a no change system, what you pick up is what you have won.
  • Items that are picked up will be quarantined immediately.

These measures could change depending on how the trial run works.

Refreshments

Everyone loves to join us for a tea and biscuit after the service however due to social distancing this will not be possible until the social distancing changes.

However, we would like to bring back some form of this.

  • To-go system with self-service, sachets and packeted biscuits.
