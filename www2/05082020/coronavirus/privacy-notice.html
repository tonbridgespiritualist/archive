Coronavirus Privacy Notice

Published 23 May 2020 (Updated 16 July 2020)

From: Office of the Secretary

Why does this service operate?

As part of Phase One of the Reopening, we created a mailing list to release updates and weekly Zoom details.

What data does this service collect?

This service collects basic information: Name and Email Address, as well as permission to send you emails via a confirmation email.

Minors aged below 13 years

If you have knowledge of an under 13 year old providing their data to this service, it is essential that you report it to the Secretary's Office who act as Data Controller, data@tonbridgespiritualist.com. If we gain knowledge of an under 13 year old providing information to our service, it will be immediately deleted.

Who holds my data?

In order to send these emails to you, ReachMail holds your data on our behalf in order to provide these emails. If you do not agree to ReachMail holding your data, it is with regret this service will be unavailable to you.

When will my data be deleted?

When the mailing list is decommissioned by order of the Secretary's Office, you will recieve an email if you signed up to the service giving you the 15 day notice of deletion. Your data will also be deleted upon request of yourself.

Recording of Zoom Services

We record our Zoom meetings to allow publication and later viewing by members. This recording is initally stored on the host computer which is property of the Chair. This video will then be edited. By using our Zoom services you give us permission to use the video which may feature your face.

From July 27th, videos will be posted to YouTube and from July 19th, the way we record Zoom services are changing.
