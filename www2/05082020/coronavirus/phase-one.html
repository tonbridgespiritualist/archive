Part of Coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic

Phase One: Moving Online

Due to the Pandemic, the Centre was forced to close because we were unable to provide the required social distance of 2m and the ban on gatherings imposed by the shutdown.

Published 23 May 2020 (Updated 18 July 2020)

From: Office of the Secretary and Jay Bragg

We are now moving online to serve our community, via Zoom. These sessions will take place on Sunday evenings. You will be emailed details at 5:45pm, provided you are signed up to the mailing list. The meeting will open at 6:10pm and we will begin at 6:30pm.

How it works

When you join the Zoom call, you will be admitted into a waiting room. If you join before the service starts, you will see a slideshow of information. When the service begins you will be muted.

When we begin, you will see Lisa open the service and then the medium will take over.

We will record the service, these will be held in accordance with our Privacy Policy, available here.

From August 3rd, these will be available to watch on YouTube or Facebook.

What You Need

  •  Zoom Video Conferencing software (on your phone, tablet or computer) (unless you want to just watch on Facebook Live)
  •  A stable internet connection
  •  To be signed up on our form below and confirm your email.

Rules

  •  Please don't have any rude or offensive backgrounds (whether real or virtual) or you risk being ejected from the meeting.
  •  Please don't be disrespectful if you receive a message, if you don't want a message please politely decline.
